[TanyaAja.in](https://www.tanyaaja.in/) is a free, ad-free platform that lets anyone collect anonymous questions from other people. Users share a personal link and anyone can submit a question without revealing their identity — a lightweight, privacy-first alternative to NGL or Sarahah.

Those existing platforms had become popular, but they came with baggage: murky data practices, intrusive ads, and a general sense that the questions you submitted were being harvested for something. I kept asking myself: what's the minimum version of this concept that you'd actually trust?

The answer turned out to be surprisingly simple — open source so anyone can audit the code, no ads, auto-delete after reading so nothing lingers in a database, and Google login so there's one less password to manage. TanyaAja.in is that version.

At its peak, the platform was used by hundreds of Indonesian creators — YouTubers, writers, and community figures who used it to collect questions from their audiences. It eventually wound down and is now archived, but it had a good run.

> **Note:** TanyaAja.in is no longer actively maintained and has been archived.

## ✨ Features

- **Anonymous submissions** — anyone with your link can ask questions without logging in or revealing who they are
- **Privacy controls** — you decide which answers are visible publicly and which stay private
- **Auto-delete** — question data is permanently deleted after you read it
- **Google sign-in** — one-click authentication, no passwords to manage
- **Public profile** — shareable page that others can visit to submit their questions
- **Ad-free** — no advertising, no distractions
- **Open source** — fully transparent codebase available on GitHub

## 📊 By the Numbers

- **872+** registered users
- **8,400+** questions submitted
- Used by hundreds of Indonesian creators at its peak
