[Baca-Quran.id](https://www.baca-quran.id/) is a free, ad-free, and privacy-respecting web app for reading the Holy Qur'an. No ads, no tracking, no analytics — just the content. It started as a personal need and grew into a fully-featured Islamic reference platform used by thousands of visitors every week.

## ✨ Features

- **Qur'an Reading** — Browse all 114 surahs or navigate verse by verse
- **Juz Amma** — Quick access to the 30th juz commonly memorized
- **Asmaul Husna** — The 99 Beautiful Names of Allah with meanings
- **Ayat Kursi** — The Throne Verse with translation
- **Do'a Harian** — Daily supplications for common occasions
- **Wirid & Dzikir** — Morning and evening remembrances (Dzikir Pagi & Petang)
- **Tasbih Counter** — Digital counter for dhikr
- **Tahlil** — Full Tahlil recitation
- **Jadwal Sholat** — Daily prayer schedule based on location
- **Pencatat Ibadah** — Personal worship tracker
- **Kalender Hijriyah** — Islamic calendar
- **Bookmarks & Favorites** — Pin favorite surahs and bookmark verses
- **Translations & Tafsir** — Indonesian translations and interpretations sourced from the Ministry of Religious Affairs

## 📝 Blog & Stories

Beyond the app itself, I also built companion content channels to support users and improve discoverability:

- **[/tulisan](https://www.baca-quran.id/tulisan/)** — A blog with articles about Islamic topics, app updates, and usage guides
- **[/stories](https://www.baca-quran.id/stories/)** — Web Stories format for quick, visual Islamic content

## 📈 SEO Impact

SEO has been one of the biggest focus areas for this project. Through continuous improvements to page structure, metadata, Core Web Vitals, structured data, and content publishing via the blog and web stories, the site has seen significant organic growth.

**Current weekly performance (Google Search Console):**

- **30.5k clicks** — up 65% week-over-week
- **208k impressions** — up 18% week-over-week

**Google Search milestones achieved:**

- Reached **60k clicks in 28 days** — February 2026
- Reached **50k clicks in 28 days** — February 2026

These numbers come from making every surah, ayat, and Islamic reference page individually indexable with proper titles, descriptions, and canonical URLs — so users searching for specific verses or prayers can land directly on the right page.
