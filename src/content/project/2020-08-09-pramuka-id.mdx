[Pramuka ID](https://pramuka-id.vercel.app/) is a digital reference hub for Indonesian Scout (Pramuka) education.

I grew up as a Scout and have fond memories of the training materials — semaphore, Morse code, the Dasa Darma principles. But when I tried to help younger members prepare for an exam, I found that the reference materials online were scattered, inconsistently worded, and sometimes outright wrong. There was no single authoritative place to look things up.

Pramuka ID is the site I wished had existed back then: everything in one place, clearly organized, and easy to pull up on a phone during a training session. It's a small project, but it fills a real gap for a community that still relies heavily on manual, printed handouts.

## ✨ Features

**Scout Materials**
- Tri Satya and Dasa Darma (Scout pledges and principles)
- Scout symbols, uniforms, and organizational structure
- Hymns and ceremonial content

**General Skills**
- Semaphore flag signaling
- Morse code and radio procedures
- Compass navigation
- Knot-tying techniques
- Special skill badge requirements

**Civics & National Identity**
- Pancasila
- Constitutional preamble (Pembukaan UUD 1945)
- Proclamation of independence

**Other**
- Downloadable reference materials
- Community contributions via GitHub — users can request new content through issues
