[Online Learning PHPID](https://learning-byphpid.netlify.app/) is a free community-driven learning platform built for the [PHPID](https://www.facebook.com/groups/phpid/) developer community in Indonesia.

When the pandemic hit in 2020, the Indonesian tech community moved online almost overnight. PHPID — one of the largest PHP developer communities in the country — suddenly had weekly live webinars running across YouTube, Zoom, and social platforms. Sessions were great, attendance was high, but once each stream ended, the recordings scattered and became hard to find again.

I built this platform as the community's shared video library: a place where every session gets indexed, stays searchable, and remains freely accessible long after the live event ends. No more hunting through YouTube channel histories or Discord archives to find that one talk someone gave three months ago.

## ✨ Features

- **218+ free webinars** — an ever-growing archive of recorded sessions available to watch anytime
- **Search by speaker** — find sessions by a specific presenter using the `from:Name` keyword format
- **Diverse tech topics** — covers web development, cloud services, backend engineering, data, IoT, business topics, and more
- **Q&A section** — community questions and answers around the learning content
- **Become a speaker** — open application for community members who want to contribute by presenting

## 🗂️ Tech Stacks

- Tailwind CSS
