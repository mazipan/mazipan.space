[Portal Satu Data](https://data.kemdikbud.go.id/) is an official open data portal for Indonesia's Ministry of Education, Culture, Research, and Technology (Kemdikbud). I was the sole web engineer on the team — responsible for taking the product from zero to one, making every frontend architecture decision along the way.

Beyond the main portal, I also built a **custom CMS from scratch** to support the editorial team managing the data and publications. And on top of that, I led the SEO strategy for the project, working to make Indonesia's national education data actually discoverable by the people who need it.

Public sector software is a different world from startup or product work. The constraints are real: strict data governance, accessibility requirements, users spread across the country on widely varying connectivity, and a need to represent official government data accurately. Getting the details wrong here isn't just a UX problem — it's a governance one. Being the only web engineer meant owning all of that end to end, which was both demanding and deeply rewarding.

## ✨ Features

- **Statistical data** — key metrics on education units, students, teachers, and facilities across Indonesia
- **Publication archive** — official reports and publications from the Ministry
- **Aggregated national data** — rolled-up figures for formal schools, non-formal education centers, and higher education
- **Data filtering** — drill down by region, education level, and other dimensions
- **Open access** — no login required to browse and explore the data

## 🗂️ Tech Stacks

- Next.js - App Router
- TanStack Query
- Internal UI Kit - based on Headless UI
- Nuqs
- Go
- Cloud SQL (PostgreSQL)
