[Portal Satu Data](https://data.kemdikbud.go.id/) is an official open data portal I built as part of my professional work with Indonesia's Ministry of Education, Culture, Research, and Technology (Kemdikbud). It provides public access to statistical data, publications, and aggregated figures covering formal and non-formal education units across the country.

Public sector software is a different world from startup or product work. The constraints are real: strict data governance, accessibility requirements, users spread across the country on widely varying connectivity, and a need to represent official government data accurately. Getting the details wrong here isn't just a UX problem — it's a governance one.

This project was one of the more challenging and rewarding engagements I've worked on. It pushed me to think carefully about performance at scale, data presentation for non-technical audiences, and what it means to build software in the public interest.

## ✨ Features

- **Statistical data** — key metrics on education units, students, teachers, and facilities across Indonesia
- **Publication archive** — official reports and publications from the Ministry
- **Aggregated national data** — rolled-up figures for formal schools, non-formal education centers, and higher education
- **Data filtering** — drill down by region, education level, and other dimensions
- **Open access** — no login required to browse and explore the data

## 🗂️ Tech Stacks

- Next.js - App Router
- TanStack Query
- Internal UI Kit - based on Headless UI
- Nuqs
- Go
- Cloud SQL (PostgreSQL)
